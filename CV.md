## Selected Products & Live Work

**Neural Draft** — Founder & CEO · https://neuraldraft.io
The backend for AI-built sites. A single API that gives AI-generated apps a production
backend: CMS, blogging, social scheduling, booking calendars, and Stripe-connected commerce —
plus an MCP server that lets AI coding tools (Claude Code, Cursor, v0) build against it with
automatic brand consistency. Multi-tenant, multi-language, with a published TypeScript SDK.

**Lex Custis** — Creator (open source) · https://lexcustis.eu · https://github.com/vbalagovic/lex-custis
Open-source, EU-sovereign compliance engine for the EU AI Act. Self-host it, point it at your
LLM, and it becomes the regulator's proof layer: a tamper-evident audit log (per-organization
HMAC-SHA256 hash chain, Art. 12), one-click Annex IV technical-documentation dossier export,
an Article 73 serious-incident workflow with SLA countdowns, PII/prompt-injection pre-checks,
and confidence/grounding/bias post-checks. Multi-tenant by design, multi-LLM (Mistral as the
EU-sovereign default or self-hosted Ollama). AGPL-3.0.

**AutoValuer** — Builder · https://autovaluer.eu
"Know what any used car is actually worth." Real-time used-car valuations computed from
266k+ live marketplace listings across 11 European markets, refreshed 4× daily. Includes
photo-based AI condition checking, fair-price ranges, and price alerts. Serves buyers,
sellers, and dealers.

**CarsDataset API** — Builder · https://api.carsdataset.com
The data engine behind AutoValuer, productized as a REST API. 54,000+ vehicles across
370+ brands (cars, trucks, motorcycles), 40+ spec fields each, plus 110,000+ price listings
from multiple countries. CSV/SQL export and search/filter/stats endpoints. Demonstrates
end-to-end scraping, normalization, and data-engineering at scale.

**Littlemind** — Builder · https://littlemindapp.com
"Understand your child, one small day at a time." A 60-second daily check-in app for parents
of children aged 4–14. After ~10 days an AI companion ("Mira") surfaces patterns in mood,
sleep, and behavior and suggests evidence-based techniques. Mobile (iOS & Android), 10
languages, therapist-ready PDF export.

**motchi.art** — Builder · https://motchi.art
An AI character-animation engine that turns inputs into character animations through an
AI-driven pipeline — aimed at creators and studios who want to skip manual animation work.
