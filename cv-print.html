Selected Products & Live Work

Neural Draft — Founder & CEO neuraldraft.io

The backend for AI-built sites. A single API giving AI-generated apps a production backend — CMS, blogging, social scheduling, booking, and Stripe-connected commerce — plus an MCP server so tools like Claude Code, Cursor and v0 build against it with automatic brand consistency. Multi-tenant, multi-language, published TypeScript SDK.

Lex Custis — Creator (open source) lexcustis.eu · github.com/vbalagovic/lex-custis

Open-source, EU-sovereign compliance engine for the EU AI Act. Self-host it, point it at your LLM, and it becomes the regulator's proof layer: a tamper-evident audit log (per-org HMAC-SHA256 hash chain), one-click Annex IV dossier export, an Article 73 serious-incident workflow, PII / prompt-injection pre-checks, and confidence / grounding / bias post-checks. Multi-tenant, multi-LLM (Mistral or self-hosted Ollama). AGPL-3.0.

AutoValuer — Builder autovaluer.eu

"Know what any used car is actually worth." Real-time used-car valuations computed from 266k+ live marketplace listings across 11 European markets, refreshed 4× daily — with photo-based AI condition checking, fair-price ranges, and price alerts.

CarsDataset API — Builder api.carsdataset.com

The data engine behind AutoValuer, productized as a REST API: 54,000+ vehicles across 370+ brands and 110,000+ price listings, with CSV/SQL export. End-to-end scraping, normalization and data engineering at scale.

Littlemind — Builder littlemindapp.com

A 60-second daily check-in app for parents of children 4–14; an AI companion surfaces patterns in mood, sleep and behavior and suggests evidence-based techniques. Mobile (iOS & Android), 10 languages, therapist-ready PDF export.

motchi.art — Builder motchi.art

An AI character-animation engine that turns inputs into character animations through an AI-driven pipeline — for creators and studios who want to skip manual animation work.
